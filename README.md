### 💡 My Focus: Enhancing the Library Ecosystem

I believe that a truly "smart" library should empower everyone within it. My self-directed research and projects focus on two main pillars:

**🎓 For Students & Patrons (User Experience & Empowerment)**
* **Data-Driven UX:** Analyzing user behavior to design intuitive and inclusive library services.
* **Smart Discovery:** Leveraging data to help students find exactly what they need, faster and more effectively.
* **AI & Digital Literacy:** Building resources to help students get comfortable with AI tools for their research and daily learning.

**🛠️ For Librarians (Efficiency & Strategic Evolution)**
* **Operational Efficiency:** Creating data-driven tools to automate repetitive tasks and streamline workflows.
* **Actionable Insights:** Visualizing complex institutional data to support evidence-based decision-making.
* **Technological Resilience:** Bridging the gap between traditional expertise and emerging tech to lead digital transformation.
